CAREER

Fred again.. started his career as a ghost producer and songwriter, working with artists like Ed Sheeran, Stormzy, and BTS. He gained recognition as a producer for Brian Eno, who mentored him. In 2021, he launched his solo project "Actual Life", a unique concept blending electronic music with real-life voice samples. His "Actual Life" album series (2021-2023) established him as a rising star in electronic music. His Boiler Room set (2022) went viral, showcasing his live improvisation skills and boosting his global fame.

He has since collaborated with big names like Swedish House Mafia, Skrillex, and Four Tet. In 2024, he continues to tour worldwide, playing major festivals and redefining modern electronic music with his emotional, sample-driven sound.
